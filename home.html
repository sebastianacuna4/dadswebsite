What We Fix

  • Iphones

    Ranging from the iphone 5 to the iphone XS Max, we work with the majority of iphone devices. And offer quick and efficient repairs, most repairs can be done same day, depending on the phone's condition.

  • Samsung Phones

    We also work with many Samsung phones, and offer several options for fixing the screen, and also work with other parts of the phone as well like the back glass, charging port, and motherboard.

  • Ipads

    We also work with many Samsung phones, and offer several options for fixing the screen, and also work with other parts of the phone as well.

  • Samsung Tablets

    For the more popular models, we offer fast repairs, and we work with most Samsung tablet models, but depending on which one it is we offer a different time frame to order the necessary component.

  • Other Android Devices

    Devices with brands like BLU, LG, Alcatel, etc. We would have to look at the specific model to be able to determine if we can fix it or not. Most of the time we can order what's needed to do the repair on the device.

  • Playstation Consoles

    For playstation consoles, we do offer some repairs. Mostly software related things like when the console is not able to turn on, needs a hard system reset, or a disk clean up.

  • Laptops

    We order laptop parts and do not have them immediately available. We offer virus removal, system cleaning, screen repairs, any other part repairs.

  • PC's

    Our repairs for laptops and PC's are the same, whatever part that needs to get fixed we would have to order. We also work with anything software related, for PC's the timeframe for repairs would be at least 4-5 days.
